HANSEL AND GRETEL

According to the Grimm Brother's tale, Hansel and Gretel are the brother and sister pair whose step-mother decides to leave them in the forest when there is a famine. Her main purpose is that they get lost and the family has enough to eat. They overhear their step-mother and gather white pebbles that they strew to find their way back. The step-mom casts them out a second time and this time they aren't so lucky. T hey keep roaming and reach a witch's house made of gingerbread and cake (the ONLY part in the story where you don't want to puke). The witch is a cannibal who eats children who cross her way. She intends to eat Hansel and Gretel too. However, they outsmart her and burn her alive in her oven. There. A child's tale full of cannibalism, child murdering mothers and witch burning children.
